Rules / Guidelines

Q. Which repositories are counted for contribution?

A. All the repositories on the DSC page are eligible for points. But the issues with the Hactoctober fest label have higher points assosiated with them.



Q. So Points only for solving the issues mentioned?

A. Your contribution can be in form of pull requests, reporting bugs, creating issues, writting tests, designing posters and others as well.



Q. I want my repository up for this contest. What should I do?

A. Each one of you who wants DSC club to promote their repositories can contact us through any medium. We would love to host your repository given that you are up there to mentain that.



Q. I still have some doubt.

A. Contact us through any of the mentioned medium.
